Apple Standard EULA Apple Privacy

1) Data collection

MemoWall does not collect or transmit personal or identifiable data to developer-operated servers. No account or login is required.

2) User content

Your notes, boards, and theme preferences are stored on your device and used only to provide the app’s features.

3) iCloud sync

If you enable iCloud sync, your content may be synced through Apple iCloud services and stored in your personal iCloud account. MemoWall does not run its own backend for note storage.

4) In-app purchases

Premium subscriptions and lifetime purchase are processed by Apple via StoreKit. MemoWall does not access payment details and only receives purchase status needed to unlock features.

5) Spatial data (visionOS)

MemoWall uses visionOS and RealityKit to render content in space. The app does not access camera video streams, eye-tracking data, or raw environmental sensor data.

6) Diagnostics & crash reports

MemoWall does not include third-party analytics or crash reporting SDKs. Apple may provide crash/diagnostic reports (e.g., via TestFlight/App Store Connect) depending on device privacy settings. These reports may contain technical information such as device model, OS version, and crash details.

7) Sharing

We do not sell personal data and do not share note contents with third parties. External processing only occurs as necessary for iCloud sync (Apple services, if enabled) and in-app purchases (Apple/StoreKit).

8) Retention & control

Your content remains until you delete it. You can delete notes/boards in the app and disable iCloud for MemoWall in system settings.

9) Contact

For privacy questions or requests: andre.finetti@gmail.com.

10) Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we’ll update the “Last updated” date.
