Quick help

  • Widget not updating? Try removing the widget and adding it again. Then restart Vision Pro.
  • iCloud sync issues? Confirm you’re signed into iCloud and that iCloud Drive is enabled. Then reopen MemoWall.
  • Premium not showing after purchase? Use “Restore Purchases” in the app, then relaunch.
  • App crashes or freezes? Send us the steps to reproduce + your device model + visionOS version.

Refunds & billing

Purchases and subscriptions are managed by Apple. For refunds or billing changes, use Apple’s purchase support flow.

If something looks wrong (missing Premium, duplicated charges), email us with your Apple ID purchase date and what you expected to happen.

FAQ

  • Do I have to open the app to use it? Not always—MemoWall includes an interactive widget designed to stay useful even without constantly opening the full app.
  • Does MemoWall collect personal data? See the Privacy Policy for details.
  • Can I suggest a feature? Yes. Email support with “Feature Request” in the subject.

Tip: Put [Bug] or [Billing] in the email subject to speed up routing.
